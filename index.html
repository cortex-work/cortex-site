Why this exists

Work moves fast.
Organizational memory does not.

Decisions get buried in chats, docs, meetings, and individual memory. Cortex Work is built to keep the connective tissue visible.

Cortex Work vs standard AI

Standard AI summarizes.

The goal is not to sound confident. The goal is to preserve decision context in a way teams can inspect, trust, and correct.
